About Me


Hello! I'm VEENA PUPPALA, a Graphologist and a Mind Coach, very much interested in enhancing soft skills among students and professionals.

I am on a mission to create 100,000 graphologists as I believe graphology can help in intensifying innate talents and igniting life skills among individuals.

As a CEO of MulteArts, I work on imparting the skills among students/working professions/TTT/Individuals using graphology. I guide them in using graphology for becoming self-aware and self-reliant in different walks of life.
