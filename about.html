Mission

My mission is to 'create 1 Lakh graphologists'. You could be wondering why I'm on this mission. It is because graphology helped me in evolving as a better person. I could evaluate myself and understand my weaknesses by which I was able to shape myself properly.
In this journey, I introduced graphology to more than ten thousand people who got benefitted from my workshops. Also, I'm proud of healing people and also empowering women and students as part of my mission. According to me, graphology can help in identifying the skill-set required for overall development for students/working professions/TTT/Individuals impacting various aspects of life.

MY STORY:

Let me share about my association with graphology and how I helped others in their development besides my growth with 'Graphology' .

I was in class IX when a graphologist visited our school. This was the first time I first heard about graphology. He said that he could read my mind looking at my handwriting. I didn't believe his words but to my surprise, he was very precise in describing me. With this incident, I was fascinated to study much about graphology.

While I was pursuing my bachelor's degree in engineering, I got in connection with graphology again. I attended a graphology course along with my friends with the thought that it would add weight to my resume. In the process of learning graphology, I started giving reports to my friends and professors. I could improve my communication skills with these interactions. This helped me in becoming confident to converse in English which I was always nervous about.

It was time for placements for our batch. It was in September, I faced an interview with Cognizant. I could create an impression in my interview and crack it with the skills I learned with the help of graphology.

I then became passionate about graphology and aimed to develop skills among individuals using graphology as a tool. I conducted my first workshop on graphology in my college. I also handled one-on-one sessions and guest lectures on graphology inspiring more than two thousand students at various schools and colleges.

In the process, my journey as 'Founder & CEO of MulteArts also started. I conducted several sessions related to graphology for personal success in MSME, iFocus, Lion club Schools, Universities and colleges, corporates and Impact in different states. I was able to impact others in developing their lives with all these sessions.

I trained around ten thousand (10K) students in imparting skills required for respective ages through multiple channels. This training helped students in developing their learning skills enabling them in cracking the interviews with more confidence.
